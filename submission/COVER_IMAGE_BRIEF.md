# MemoryBridge — Cover Image Brief

This document serves as a creative brief for generating or designing the main project cover image for the Kaggle Agents for Good submission.

## Objective
Create a welcoming, serene, and professional cover image that visually communicates "safe daily support and connection" between a caregiver and an individual with cognitive decline.

## Mandatory Constraints (Safety & Ethics)
- **NO Medical Claims:** The image must not contain medical symbols (crosses, stethoscopes, brain scans, pills) that imply diagnosis or medical treatment.
- **NO Exploitative Imagery:** Do not use dark, frightening, or stereotypical depictions of dementia (e.g., people holding their heads in despair, fading puzzle pieces, isolated individuals in dark rooms).
- **Positive Framing:** The tone must be uplifting, respectful, and focused on *support and connection*, rather than *disease and monitoring*.

## Visual Concepts

**Concept 1: The Digital Bridge**
A warm, abstract representation of two people (perhaps stylized or in silhouette) connected by a gentle, glowing bridge. The color palette should use calming blues, soft greens, and warm yellows. The text "MemoryBridge" is prominent, with the subtitle "Safe Daily Routines".

**Concept 2: The Accessible UI in Context**
A clean, blurred background of a cozy, sunlit living room. In the foreground, a highly stylized, accessible tablet UI showing a simple "Morning Tea" routine with a friendly checkmark. The "Agents for Good" logo is subtly integrated into the bottom corner.

**Concept 3: Human Connection**
A high-quality, diverse stock photo (or high-fidelity AI generation) of a younger caregiver and an older adult sharing a warm moment, perhaps looking at a tablet together or drinking tea. Overlay the MemoryBridge logo and a soft color gradient to provide space for the title text.

## Technical Requirements
- **Dimensions:** 16:9 aspect ratio (e.g., 1920x1080).
- **Format:** PNG or high-quality JPG.
- **Typography:** If text is included, use modern, highly legible sans-serif fonts (e.g., Inter, Roboto).
- **Accessibility:** Ensure high contrast if text overlays images.
